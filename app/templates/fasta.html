DeePNAP

Enter your Text in the FASTA format.

The web portal can parse through at most 20 Sequences

Make sure the number of Proteins and Nucleic Acid Sequences are equal.

The first protein is mapped to the First Nucleic Acid sequence. The Second is mapped to the second, etc etc.

Once you press Generate Output, you will be able to download the output as a CSV file.
