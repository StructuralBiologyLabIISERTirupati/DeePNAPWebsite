DeePNAP

Check Your Protein-Nucleic Acid Binding Affinity with DeePNAP

Check Your Protein-Nucleic Acid Binding Affinity with DeePNAP

Inputs Required: Single Protein Sequence, and a Single Nucleic Acid Sequence

DeePNAP is a deep learning model with over 170,000 parameters that has been trained on 14,400 experimentally confirmed P-NA complex binding affinity data to estimate KD for the given P and NA sequence.

Citation

Uddeshya Pandey, Sasi Mitra Behara, Debasish Koner and Hussain Bhukya "DeePNAP: A deep learning method to predict protein-nucleic acids binding affinity from sequence"
